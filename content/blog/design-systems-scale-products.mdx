# Design Systems: Scaling Products Without Chaos

As products grow, inconsistency becomes the biggest hidden cost.

Different screens, different styles, and repeated decisions slow down teams.

---

## What a Design System Solves

- UI inconsistency  
- repeated design decisions  
- slow development cycles  
- poor scalability  

---

## Core Elements

- typography system  
- color tokens  
- spacing rules  
- reusable components  
- interaction patterns  

---

## Impact on Teams

Design systems help:

- designers move faster  
- developers reuse components  
- products scale consistently  

---

## Key Takeaways

- consistency = speed  
- systems reduce decision fatigue  
- scalable products require structured design
