# When to Redesign Your Product for Better Performance

Redesigning a product is expensive. Doing it at the wrong time is even more expensive.

---

## Signs You Need a Redesign

- low user engagement  
- high drop-off rates  
- inconsistent UI  
- user confusion  
- scaling issues  

---

## When NOT to Redesign

- no product-market fit yet  
- problem is not validated  
- data is insufficient  

---

## Better Approach

Instead of full redesign:

- fix key flows  
- improve onboarding  
- enhance clarity  

---

## Key Takeaways

- redesign should be data-driven  
- fix problems before redesigning everything  
- incremental improvements often work better
