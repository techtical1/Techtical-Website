# Top UX Mistakes That Reduce Product Engagement

Many products lose users not because of features, but because of poor experience decisions.

---

## Common UX Mistakes

### 1. Overcomplicated onboarding  
Too many steps create friction.

### 2. Weak visual hierarchy  
Users cannot scan or understand content quickly.

### 3. Poor CTA clarity  
Users do not know what to do next.

### 4. No feedback system  
Actions feel disconnected without responses.

### 5. Inconsistent UI  
Creates confusion and reduces trust.

---

## Fixing These Issues

Focus on:

- simplicity  
- clarity  
- consistency  
- feedback  

---

## Key Takeaways

- small UX mistakes compound over time  
- clarity improves engagement instantly  
- fixing UX often gives faster results than adding features
